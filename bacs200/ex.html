Strategies

Dumb Friends League's plan is simple and focuses on four major issues regarding animals. Dumb Friends League prevents abuse and neglect toward animals, promotes adoption for pets, prevents homeless pets, provides pre-adoption care.

Getting Invloved

There are all kinds of ways to join Dumb Friends League organization and support their cause. Dumb Friends League accepts donations of cash, food, and household pet items, to donate to Dumb Friends League's cause use the following link.Donate. Other ways you can support the Dumb Friends League is through adopting pets or volunterring at shelters.
